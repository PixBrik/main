## Product promise

Fotobrik turns a photo into a buildable interpretation made from interlocking brick pieces, then explains the design, parts, alternatives, and possible purchase routes for the user's country.

The MVP must not promise an exact replica, structural certification, current stock, or a complete physical-store inventory. Every result must disclose whether it came from validated catalog data, an estimate, a fixture, or a time-stamped observation.

## Working vocabulary

- **Design:** a shape or mould independent of colour.
- **Element:** a purchasable or observed design variant, including colour and, when known, material or decoration.
- **Build:** a Fotobrik-generated arrangement plus a bill of materials.
- **Offer observation:** a seller, price, quantity, country, condition, and observation time. It is not permanent availability.
- **Catalog run:** one traceable import attempt with a source version and digest.
- **Release:** an immutable, approved view used to reproduce a build. Formal release records are a planned layer above the current run-based foundation.
